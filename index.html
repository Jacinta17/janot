About Janot

Janot Restaurant, located in Panaji, Goa, offers an exceptional dining experience in a contemporary setting. The menu features a creative twist on traditional Goan cuisine, showcasing re-imagined dishes that highlight the region's rich culinary heritage. Guests can also indulge in expertly crafted Feni cocktails, making it a perfect destination for both locals and visitors seeking a unique gastronomic adventure.

Chef Avinash Martins

Celebrated for his deep-rooted love for Goan traditions and culinary innovation, Chef Avinash Martins brings authenticity and artistry to every dish. At Janot, his refined approach to local cuisine redefines how guests experience Goan flavors.

About Janot

Janôt is an old Portuguese expression an exclamation of beauty and delight. It’s a word Chef Avinash heard often from his grandmother, now lovingly revived through this restaurant. Inhonoring that memory, Janôt becomes a space where tradition meets imagination. Our approach to food is intentionally fluid. Unbound by cuisine,yet rooted in a deep reverence for ingredients, we allow theseasons, the land, and the stories behind each element to guideus. Every dish is a thoughtful expression personal, place-driven,and crafted with care.

Chef Avinash Martins

Celebrated for his deep-rooted love for Goan traditions and culinary innovation, Chef Avinash Martins brings authenticity and artistry to every dish. At Janot, his refined approach to local cuisine redefines how guests experience Goan flavors.
